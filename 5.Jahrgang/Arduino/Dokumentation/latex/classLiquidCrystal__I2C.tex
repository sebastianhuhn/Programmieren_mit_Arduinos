\subsection{Ausführliche Beschreibung}
This is the driver for the Liquid Crystal L\+CD displays that use the I2C bus.

After creating an instance of this class, first call \hyperlink{classLiquidCrystal__I2C_a62235d63b74abf26160d7cfe67a9687e}{begin()} before anything else. The backlight is on by default, since that is the most likely operating mode in most cases.
